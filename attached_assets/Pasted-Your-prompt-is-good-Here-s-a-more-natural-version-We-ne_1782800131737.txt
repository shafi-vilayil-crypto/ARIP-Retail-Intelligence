I like the visual language of the screenshot you shared, but I would treat it as design inspiration only, not something to replicate.

The design system should feel like a premium SaaS analytics product.

* Large whitespace

* Floating cards with subtle elevation
* Clean typography
* Minimal icons

* Responsive grid
* Progressive disclosure (show details on demand)

The Platform Should Be Company-Agnostic

This is one of the most important architectural decisions.

The system should not contain any Mouzy-specific logic.

Instead, every analysis is driven by a Company Profile object.

That means the same platform can analyze:

* Mouzy
* Milma
* Amul
* Starbucks
* Domino’s
* McDonald’s
* KFC
* Reliance Retail
* Third Wave Coffee
* Any future retail chain

without changing the code.
